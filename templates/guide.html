How to create an online portfolio

  1. Gather inspiration
  2. Choose a template
  3. Showcase your best projects
  4. Use high quality images
  5. Include the right content and features
  6. Make it mobile friendly
  7. Ask for honest feedback

01. Gather inspiration

Creating a portfolio begins with some visual research. Whichever field you’re in, looking around at other online portfolio websites will provide you with plenty of inspiration and generate some initial ideas. It’s especially recommended to take a look at websites by other people in your specific creative niche or industry.
